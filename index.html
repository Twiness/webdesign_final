PulsNa is a non-profit independent space education agency and united presitigious research centers, agencys for LIGO, SapacX, NASA and AIRC.

PulsNa aims at educating people on astrophysics, aerospace engineering through real-life experiment and share knowledge around the world. In addition, we preparing emerging leaders in the field of physics and engineer around the globe.

The mission of PulsNa is to ensure peaceful coporation among various research centers and agencys to provide the best perspective of outer space.
